Titanic

This movie is about a romance between Rose and Jack on the Titanic. They go through many obstacles together. It's a fiction based story but it makes the watchers think that it's real because it is based on the true ship, Titanic.

There a two main characters in the movie, Rose and Jack.

Rose has red curly hair, blue eyes, and is a strong and confident woman. Jack has brown hair with hazel eyes. He is the protective, strong person in the movie.

Jack's love for Rose was unbreakable and so faithful. One of his quotes that prove that is when he says, "Winning that ticket, Rose was the best thing that ever happened to me...it brought me to you...You must do me this honor, Rose. Promise me you'll survive. That you won't give up, no matter what happens, no matter how hopeless. Promise me now, Rose, and never let go of that promise."
