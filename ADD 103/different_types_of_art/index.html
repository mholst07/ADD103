What Is Art?

Art is a special way people express ideas, emotions, and imagination. It has existed for thousands of years, from early cave drawings to modern digital designs. Art can make us think, feel, and connect with others. Some art is visual, like paintings and sculptures. Other art involves sound and movement, like music or dance. Every culture in the world creates art that reflects its people and traditions. This website explores five main types of art to show how creativity appears in many forms around us.
