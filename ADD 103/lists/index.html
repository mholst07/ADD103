Steps To Wash Dishes

  1. Rinse off food debris from dishes with hot water.
  2. Use hot water and dish soap on a spong to scrub the dish.
  3. Rinse off all the soap that is leftover on the dish.
  4. Dry it manually with a towel or put it in a drying rack.
